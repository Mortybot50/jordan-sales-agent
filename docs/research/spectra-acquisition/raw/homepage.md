## What Counts as an "Opportunity"

"An 'opportunity' is not a random reply. Not 'sounds interesting.' Not a tire-kicker who wants pricing.

A Qualified Opportunity = someone who:
- Matches the ICP you approve
- Is a decision maker (Founder/CEO/Partner/Head of BD)
- Has the economics to buy (budget / retainer capability)
- Signals real intent (pain + openness to next step)
- Gets entered into your pipeline as an Opportunity (this is what the contract anchors to)

This is how we protect quality — and why we can align to revenue."
