## Featured Case Studies (detailed)

### Inc. 5000 Business Funding Company — $620K Closed In 14 Days
- **Challenge:** Needed to rapidly scale outreach to SMB founders seeking capital.
- **Strategy:** Built outbound campaign targeting founder-led companies experiencing growth constraints (funding for expansion, cash flow bottlenecks, quick approval timelines).
- **Results:** 50 Meetings Booked / 14 Days / $620,000+ Revenue Closed

### Home Leadz — Local Marketing Agency — $340K Pipeline Generated
- **Challenge:** Wanted to scale outreach to home service businesses nationwide.
- **Strategy:** High-volume outbound campaign with 800 sending accounts and highly targeted messaging.
- **Results:** 94,400 Emails Sent / 5.4% Reply Rate / 206 Meetings Booked / $340,000 LTV Pipeline

### Inbox Accelerator — SaaS — 38 Meetings & 10 Customers
- **Challenge:** Needed a scalable acquisition channel for agencies and SaaS founders.
- **Strategy:** High-volume campaign targeting marketing agencies and SaaS founders using 400 sending accounts.
- **Results:** 38 Meetings / 10 Customers / Timeline: 15 Days

### AirClinic AI — Healthcare Technology — 28 Meetings In 14 Days
- **Challenge:** Connect with dental practice owners interested in AI automation.
- **Strategy:** Outbound campaign targeting practice owners and dental groups across the US.
- **Results:** 28 Meetings Booked / Timeline: 14 Days

### Bowarr — Private Buyer — Acquisition Sourced Via Cold Email
- **Challenge:** Needed access to off-market healthcare businesses for acquisition.
- **Strategy:** Outbound campaign targeting assisted living owners and healthcare operators.
- **Results:** 27 Meetings / 1 Acquisition Completed

## Why It Works (their framing)
"Across hundreds of outbound campaigns we've identified a consistent pattern behind successful campaigns:
- Precise targeting of ideal prospects
- Messaging that focuses on results
- Personalized outreach
- Structured follow-ups across multiple channels

Most campaigns begin generating meetings within 7–14 days."
