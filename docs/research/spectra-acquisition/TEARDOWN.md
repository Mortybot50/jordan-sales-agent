## 4. Workflow Deep-Dives

### Workflow 1: Onboarding (Weeks 1–2)
1. Client books strategy call via GoHighLevel booking widget
2. Strategy call: define ICP, messaging pillars, revenue targets
3. Spectra builds infrastructure: registers domains, provisions 1,000 sending inboxes
4. Domains warm up (automated email sending to seed accounts to build reputation)
5. ICP workshop: define decision-maker profile, company criteria, budget signals
6. Data sourcing: Spectra pulls lists from Apollo/SalesNav/Outscraper/Clay
7. List verification (email validation, bounce scrubbing)
8. Copywriting: Spectra writes email sequences per ICP segment

### Workflow 2: Campaign Launch (Weeks 3–4)
1. Email sequences activated across 1,000 inboxes
2. LinkedIn sequences launched simultaneously (connection requests + messages)
3. A/B tests run across subject lines, CTAs, angles
4. Routing configured: positive replies → client calendar booking
5. Calendar integration: auto-booking to client's calendar (Calendly or GHL)
6. Tracking: reply rates, booking rates, qualified opp rate monitored

### Workflow 3: Ongoing Lead Management
1. Spectra's team reads every reply
2. Qualified leads → meeting booked to client's calendar
3. Unqualified → automated follow-up or suppressed
4. No-shows → rescheduling sequences triggered
5. Warm but not ready → reactivation queue for future campaigns
6. Warm calling team calls high-intent replies before meeting

### Workflow 4: Opportunity Handoff to Client
1. Spectra enters qualified opportunity into client's pipeline (GoHighLevel-based)
2. Client sees deal in their pipeline — this is the contractual deliverable
3. Client shows up to the meeting and closes the deal
4. Spectra tracks close → collects revenue share %

### Workflow 5: Monthly Review
1. KPI review call: qualified opps generated, show-up rate, close rate, rev share earned
2. Spectra proposes optimisations: new segments, revised copy, new channels
3. Scaling strategies discussed

**Key observation:** Client interaction with any software is likely minimal. This is a managed service, not a tool. The pipeline dashboard (if any) is likely read-only, delivered via GoHighLevel white-label or a PDF report.
