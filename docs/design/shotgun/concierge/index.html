Direction 03

Concierge

A CRM that asserts its power through restraint. One thing on screen at a time. Huge quiet space. Typography does the work that chrome usually does.

The anti-Attio. Instead of showing the operator every field, every tag, every badge, Concierge shows the single next decision — approve the draft, work the hot column, reply to Sarah — and trusts that everything else can wait a minute longer.

Pro  Calm, focused, beautiful. Operators who live in their inbox will feel at home — the product recedes and the work comes forward. Pairs naturally with keyboard-first use.

Con  Slower for raw data-entry work. Hides the density some operators want — the "show me every open deal and every field" crowd will find it precious. Not for teams who grade themselves on activity volume.
